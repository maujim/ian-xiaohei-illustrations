# Style DNA

## One Sentence

Pure white, minimal, hand-drawn, spacious, restrained, absurd, product-sketch-like, with handwritten English labels; clear structure without feeling like a manual.

Like an explanatory sketch casually drawn on blank white paper by someone who has spent years working on AI, products, design, and developer tools.

## Must-Haves

- 16:9 horizontal article illustration.
- Pure white background: no beige, warm gray, paper texture, gradients, shadows, noise, or vintage-paper feel.
- Mostly black hand-drawn line art: thin lines, slightly wobbly, not mechanical, not vector-like, no heavy outlines.
- Lots of white space: the main subject should occupy about 40%-60% of the canvas, with at least 35% empty space and ideally one fully quiet area.
- A small number of handwritten English annotations: at most 5-8 labels, each preferably 2-8 words.
- One image explains only one core action, structure, state, or metaphor.
- Express the structure naturally; do not write the structure type name on the image.

## Color

- Black: main line art, characters, frames, structures, primary text, and main objects.
- Red: emphasis notes, problems, emotional points, key reminders, and outcomes.
- Orange: main flow, paths, arrows, automation direction, and movement from A to B.
- Blue: supplemental explanations, mental states, system states, second-layer explanations, and AI/assistant/automation hints.

Blue is not required in every image. Use color with restraint; less is better than more.

## Absolutely Avoid

- No commercial illustration.
- No PPT infographic.
- No formal flowchart.
- No course slide.
- No cute cartoon poster.
- No children's illustration.
- No complex architecture diagram.
- No polished flat illustration.
- No tech-style UI.
- No real app screenshot.
- No complex backgrounds, gradients, shadows, or textures.
- Do not explain every node clearly.
- Do not write type titles in the top-left corner, such as "Workflow Diagram / System Architecture Diagram / Common Pitfalls / Roadmap".

## Aesthetic Direction

Make it absurd, creative, interesting, clean, refreshing, and imaginative.

Do not make it cute, childish, overly complex, or stiff.
